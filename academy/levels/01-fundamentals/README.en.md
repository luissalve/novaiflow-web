# Level 1 · Fundamentals — Overview


*Getting started with AI — your first useful prompts, together.*

**From zero to your first useful prompt — in one hour.** This is the entry point of NovAIFlow's AI
Training & Enablement path. No prior AI experience, no coding, no jargon. By the end you will understand
what generative AI really is, know the simple formula for talking to it, and walk away with **3 prompts

## Learning objectives

By the end of Level 1, every participant can:

1. **Explain what generative AI is** — and how it differs from a web search — in their own words.
2. **Understand how a chat assistant works**, including what it can and can't do (and that it can be
   confidently wrong).
3. **Write a clear prompt** using the **Role · Task · Context · Format** formula.
4. **Refine instead of restart** — improve an answer by steering the conversation.
5. **Apply the three golden rules** of responsible use (protect data, human-in-the-loop, verify facts).
6. **Produce 3 real prompts** tied to their own daily work.

## Outcomes — what you leave with

- A working mental model of generative AI, free of hype and jargon.
- The **Role · Task · Context · Format** formula, practiced live.
- **3 saved prompts** for your real tasks — your graded deliverable.
- A one-page cheat-sheet and a plain-language glossary to keep.
- The three responsible-use habits that keep AI safe at work.
- Your **Level 1 certificate** once the deliverable meets the rubric.

---

## Your deliverable

Three useful prompts for your own role, each built with the prompt formula, tested, and free of
sensitive data. This is what earns your certificate. The full brief and acceptance criteria are in the
project folder, and grading is in the assessment folder.

> **Responsible AI, from day one.** Never paste sensitive data (PHI/PII/confidential) into a public tool
> without an approved policy. AI drafts — a person reviews and owns the result. And always verify facts
> and figures before using them.
